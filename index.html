About Us

We are Joshua and Manuel - two friends who share a passion for fitness and a healthy lifestyle. Joshua, from Cape Town, has years of experience in the nutritional supplements and health products industry in South Africa. Manuel is originally from Europe and has already established and managed companies with a focus on import/export and logistics in the past.

Together we have made it our mission to introduce some of Europe's most popular and innovative protein-rich foods to the South African market -- starting with Germany's popular high-protein milk.
